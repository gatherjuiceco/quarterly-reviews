Patterns & Themes

The system compounds

Kiosk: idea → live in 8 weeks. COGS: named out loud to Clayton June 9 → 8-task sprint → fixed by June 27. Saying numbers to someone with a stake works.

Structure deepened, not just held

Daily planning appeared June 1 and ran all 30 days. Biweekly Clayton cadence established. Counseling started weekly despite being "deferred."

June-the-kid goals keep dying

Playdate infrastructure, the party, playgroups — missed or dropped across 4+ consecutive periods, spanning two quarters. The only domain with a repeating failure signature.

Cardio quietly exited

"Strength & Zone 2" became just "Strength" on the calendar by late May. Lifts are at all-time highs; the aerobic half of the fitness framework has no goal, no block, no data.

Where Time Went

April — Opening

Inspection, production days, store setup, floor time. Biz Dev blocks emerge in week 3 — ON work started 2 weeks after doors opened.

May — Building

"On Time" and Office Hours blocks dominate. Kiosk install, ESB defense sprint, LTO launch, marketing engine buildout. One friend hang (Alex, May 19).

June — Events & Accountability

Three community events, two Clayton business meetings, weekly counseling, daily planning every day. Then June 20–27: six floor shifts (~34 hrs) covering the staffing gap.
